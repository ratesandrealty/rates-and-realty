Who We Are

Built by Borrowers, for Borrowers

We started Rates & Realty after experiencing firsthand how broken the mortgage process could be — slow response times, mountains of paperwork, and loan officers who disappeared after the application was submitted.

Our mission is simple: use technology to make homebuying faster and less stressful, while keeping the human expertise that matters most when you're making the biggest financial decision of your life.
