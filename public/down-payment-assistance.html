CalHFA Dream For All
First-Gen Priority
Up to $150,000
Shared appreciation loan

California's flagship DPA — up to 20% of purchase price (max $150,000) as a shared appreciation loan. No monthly payments ever. Repay original amount plus a share of appreciation when you sell or refinance. Lottery-based — program opens in short windows. First-generation, first-time homebuyers only.

Min Credit660 FICO
Buyer TypeFirst-generation only
IncomeCalHFA county limits
AvailabilityStatewide CA
💡 Pro tip: Dream For All sells out within hours of opening. Get on Rene's alert list now — you need a pre-approval ready before the next round.

CalHFA MyHome Assistance
Most Popular
Up to $15,000
Deferred-payment second mortgage

A deferred "silent second" loan for down payment and/or closing costs — up to 3.5% for FHA loans or 3% for Conventional. No monthly payments. Nothing is due until you sell, refinance, or pay off your first mortgage. Works with CalHFA FHA, Conventional, VA, and USDA loans.

Min Credit660 FICO
Buyer TypeFirst-time only
Loan TypesFHA, Conv, VA, USDA
AvailabilityStatewide CA
💡 Pro tip: Stack MyHome with CalHFA ZIP to cover both your down payment AND closing costs — you may need very little cash at closing.

GSFA Platinum Program
No First-Time Req
Up to 5.5%
Grant — never repaid

One of the only CA programs with no first-time homebuyer requirement. Up to 5.5% of loan amount as a combination grant (never repaid) + optional second mortgage. Works with FHA and Conventional. Special "Platinum SELECT" tier for healthcare workers, teachers, and public safety employees.

Min Credit640 FICO
Buyer TypeFirst-time + Repeat
IncomeNo strict cap
AvailabilityStatewide CA
💡 Pro tip: Owned a home in the last 3 years? GSFA Platinum may be your only option — and it's a strong one. Ask Rene about the SELECT tier if you work in healthcare or education.

CalHFA ZIP
Closing Costs
Up to 3%
Zero-interest deferred loan

Zero-interest deferred second loan covering closing costs up to 3% of the loan amount. Pairs exclusively with CalPLUS first mortgage programs. No monthly payments, no interest ever. Combine with MyHome to cover both down payment and closing costs in a single transaction.

Min Credit660 FICO
Buyer TypeFirst-time only
CoversClosing costs only
Pairs WithCalPLUS FHA/Conv
💡 Pro tip: ZIP + MyHome together is Rene's most-used combo for first-time buyers — it can cut your out-of-pocket cash at closing dramatically.

OC MAP Grant
Orange County
Up to $80,000
Forgivable grant (5-year)

Orange County's own Mortgage Assistance Program. Up to $80,000 as a 30-year deferred loan at 3% simple interest — no monthly payments until you sell, refinance, or transfer. Income must be at or below 80% Area Median Income. Must purchase in unincorporated OC or participating city.

Min Credit620 FICO
Buyer TypeFirst-time only
Income≤ 80% AMI
AvailabilityOrange County only
💡 Pro tip: County-funded programs have limited dollars and can run out mid-year. Start this process early — Rene knows which cities are currently participating.

Santa Ana DPA
City Program
Up to $40,000
Deferred silent second

City of Santa Ana offers up to $40,000 at zero interest — no interest ever accrues. First-come, first-served basis for residents and workers buying within Santa Ana city limits. A HUD-approved homebuyer education course is required. Funds open in waves and close quickly.

Min Credit620 FICO
InterestZero — never accrues
RequirementLive/work in Santa Ana
EducationHUD course required
💡 Pro tip: Santa Ana DPA funds open and close fast. If you're buying anywhere in Santa Ana, mention it to Rene on your very first call — timing is everything.
