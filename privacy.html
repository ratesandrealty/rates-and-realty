Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Quick summary: We collect the information you give us when you inquire about a mortgage. We use it to evaluate your loan scenario, communicate with you about it, and provide our brokerage services. We don't sell your personal information. You can ask us to delete it. 2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you submit a loan inquiry or apply for a mortgage, we collect information you provide, including:

  • Identifiers: name, address, phone number, email, date of birth, Social Security number (when required for credit and underwriting)
  • Financial information: income, employment, assets, debts, credit history, bank statements, pay stubs, tax documents, and other materials required for loan underwriting
  • Property information: address of the property being financed, purchase price, value, intended use
  • Communication records: emails, text messages, and call notes between you and Rates & Realty staff

Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, we collect limited technical information automatically, including IP address, browser type, device type, referring URL, and the pages you view. We may use cookies and similar technologies for basic site functionality and analytics.

Information from third parties

With your consent, we may receive information from credit bureaus, employment verification services, banks (for asset verification), title companies, and other parties involved in your loan transaction.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To evaluate, prepare, and process your mortgage loan application
  • To match you to suitable loan programs and pricing
  • To communicate with you about your loan inquiry or active loan file (via phone, SMS, and email)
  • To comply with our legal and regulatory obligations as a licensed mortgage broker (including those of the California DRE, NMLS, and federal lending laws)
  • To maintain internal records, prevent fraud, and improve our services
  • To provide pre-approval letters, fee sheets, and other transaction documents

We do not use your personal information for advertising to consumers outside our brokerage, and we do not sell your personal information.

4. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only as needed to provide our services and as required by law. This may include:

  • Wholesale and correspondent lenders: when we submit your loan file for underwriting and pricing
  • Credit bureaus and verification services: for credit reports, employment verification, and asset verification
  • Title companies, escrow companies, and settlement agents: for closing your loan
  • Real estate agents and other transaction parties: when relevant to your transaction
  • Government regulators and authorities: when required by law (DRE, NMLS, IRS, etc.)
  • Service providers: who help us operate our business (e.g., cloud hosting, CRM tools), under written confidentiality obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. SMS / Text Message Communications

We use SMS text messaging in two distinct ways:

  • Client communications: If you are an active loan applicant and have provided your phone number, we may text you about your loan file. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP, or by emailing us at rene@ratesandrealty.com. Standard message and data rates may apply.
  • Internal staff SMS assistant: Rates & Realty operates an internal AI-powered SMS assistant for use by the licensed loan officer and authorized admin staff only. This service is not consumer-facing. Only phone numbers manually added to an internal whitelist by the system administrator can interact with it. If you are not authorized and have texted this number by mistake, your message receives an automated rejection notice and is not processed further.

Message frequency for active loan applicants varies depending on the stage of your loan and your preferences. We never share your phone number with third parties for marketing.

6. Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell (we do not sell)
  • Right to delete personal information we have collected, subject to legal retention obligations
  • Right to correct inaccurate personal information
  • Right to opt out of sale or sharing of personal information (we do not engage in either)
  • Right to limit use of sensitive personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise any of these rights, email rene@ratesandrealty.com with your request. We may need to verify your identity before processing the request. We will respond within 45 days, as required by law.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as needed to provide our services and to comply with federal and state recordkeeping requirements for mortgage brokerage activity. After applicable retention periods expire, we securely delete or anonymize the information.

8. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. We do not transmit Social Security numbers, full dates of birth, or bank account numbers over SMS.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.
