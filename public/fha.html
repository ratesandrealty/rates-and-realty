FAQ

Common Questions

What is FHA mortgage insurance (MIP)?

FHA loans require an upfront MIP (1.75% of loan amount) and an annual MIP (0.55%–1.05% depending on term and LTV). MIP remains for the life of the loan if down payment is less than 10%.

Can I use gift funds for the down payment?

Yes. FHA allows 100% of the down payment to come from gift funds from a family member, employer, or approved non-profit organization.

Can I buy a multi-family with FHA?

Yes — FHA allows purchase of 2–4 unit properties as long as you occupy one of the units as your primary residence. You can use rental income from other units to help qualify.

How long after bankruptcy or foreclosure can I get an FHA loan?

Chapter 7 bankruptcy: 2-year waiting period. Chapter 13: 1 year with court permission. Foreclosure: 3-year waiting period from completion date.
