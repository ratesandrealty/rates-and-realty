FAQ

Common Questions

What types of commercial properties do you finance?

Office, retail, industrial, mixed-use, multifamily (5+ units), self-storage, hospitality, and special purpose properties. Through RFD Group we also connect borrowers with private capital and bridge lenders.

What's the minimum loan amount for commercial?

Most commercial lenders start at $500,000. Some community banks go lower. Bridge and private capital lenders typically start at $1M+.

How is commercial underwriting different from residential?

Commercial loans qualify primarily on the property's income (DSCR), not your personal income. Lenders look at NOI, occupancy, lease terms, and market conditions alongside your credit and net worth.

What loan terms are available?

Typically 5, 7, or 10-year fixed periods with 20–30 year amortization. Bridge loans are 12–36 months. SBA 504 loans go up to 25 years.

How long does commercial loan approval take?

30–60 days for most conventional commercial loans. Bridge and private capital can close in 2–3 weeks with a complete package.
