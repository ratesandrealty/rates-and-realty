Common Questions

Will this affect my credit score? +
Not at all. This form only does a soft inquiry to get you started — zero impact to your credit score. A hard pull only happens when you formally authorize it, and we'll always ask first.
How fast can I get pre-approved? +
Most clients get a pre-approval letter the same business day once we receive your basic documents (pay stubs, bank statements, ID). Rene reviews every file personally and responds fast.
What if my credit isn't perfect? +
FHA loans start at 580 credit score. We also have bank statement loans for self-employed borrowers, DSCR loans for investors, and a $97 credit optimization analysis that helps most clients improve 20–80 points in 30–45 days.
Do I need 20% down? +
Absolutely not. FHA requires as little as 3.5% down, conventional can be as low as 3%, VA loans require $0 down for eligible veterans, and we have down payment assistance programs that can cover your entire down payment.
I'm self-employed — can I still qualify? +
Yes. Bank statement loans let you qualify on 12–24 months of deposits instead of tax returns — no W-2s required. Perfect for business owners, freelancers, and 1099 earners whose write-offs reduce their taxable income.
